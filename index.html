About Me

Hi, I'm Sean. I'm a Web Developer and Designer living in Fort Lauderdale. My passion for development and design started at a young age, when I taught myself HTML by reading the source code of awesome 90s web sites. I worked for a newspaper in high school, where I learned Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. I quickly and became obsessed with perfect presentation. I went to college, worked in retail, and lived in New York City. At the beginning of 2016, I decided to pursue my love of creatology further and attended Wyncode's Web Immersive boot camp. Check out my portfolio, and let's create some forward thinking, responsive, beautiful projects together.
