About Me

Hi, I'm Sean. I'm a Junior Fullstack Web Developer and Designer living in Fort Lauderdale. My passion for development and design started at a young age, when I used to read the the source codes of basic HTML sites in the 90s. I taught myself the fundamentals of HTML as they existed at the time (and much of which is now depreciated). I ran a few websites on Geocities and had a lot of fun. During my senior year of high school, I was hired to design ads and paginate for the local hometown newspaper. I learned Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign quickly and became obsessed with making sure everything was presented perfectly.

I decided to study Graphic Design in college, but found that I didn’t enjoy the traditional approach as much and would have preferred a more digital-based curriculum. I didn’t know what to do next and moved into retail. In 2009, I relocated to New York and worked at the Apple Store on 5th Avenue and in SoHo for a number of years. I knew I wanted to focus more on the tech and creative spectrum, and was offered a job as a recruiter sourcing developers and designers. While I learned a lot about the industry, I quickly realized recruiting was not for me.

I had some knowledge of code schools and began researching various opportunities. I came across Wyncode, and having family in Fort Lauderdale and feeling lost in New York, I decided to move back. I’ve greatly enjoyed my time at Wyncode and learned far more than I could ever fathom in such a short time. I’m hoping to find an opportunity that allows me to utilize my creative and technology knowledge.

Thanks for visiting my site! I hope you enjoy it and I look forward to any feedback.
